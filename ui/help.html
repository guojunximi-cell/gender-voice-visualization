Tutorial

Mobile users: You can tap the top edge of this pane to expand/collapse it for easier reading.

This app visualizes the pitch and resonance of voices in recordings over time.

Typically, voices perceived as female have brighter resonance and higher pitch.

Press the play button to play the clip associated with the selected point on the graph.

Your clips are sent to our server for processing, but they are not stored persistently.

Good luck!

FAQ

Why does the marker move around so much?

Most speech has a broad range of values in pitch and resonance.

Why do all the markers start so close together?

When not playing, the markers are displayed at the median values across the whole clip.

Does this program support languages other than English?

English and Mandarin Chinese are both supported.

Do accent and dialect affect pitch and resonance?

Yes.

Ok, I can see my resonance now, but how do I actually change it?

There are several ways.

Your resonance is also affected by the space in your mouth.

Even though my pitch and resonance seem to be in the right spot, I don't feel like my voice sounds like I want it to.

There are many dimensions to voice other than pitch and resonance.
